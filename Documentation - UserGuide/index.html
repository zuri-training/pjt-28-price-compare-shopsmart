NEW USER
As a new user, you can get access to

  • basic product information
  • pricing
  • reviews
  • browse through available products
However to gain access to additional features, as a first time user,you must first register with either your email address and password or via one of the following methods: Google account, Facebook account, or Twitter account.
REGISTERED USER
    As a registered user, you have full access to the website. Here are actions you can take
  • Look up product history
    • Look up product history
  • See similar product recommendations.
  • Leave a comment under the product/price.
  • Add products to watch list and set a price change alert.
  • Share on social media and email.
  • Have access to the purchase page.
  • Write and view reviews
  • View full product details
  • View available sellers for the desired product.
